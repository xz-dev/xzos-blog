## 1.Kindle Standby and Wakeup:

- With Kindle case functionality, closing the cover locks the screen, opening it wakes the device.
- Alternatively, press the button at the bottom of the Kindle to sleep/wake.

**Kindle consumes very little power in standby mode, so shutting down is unnecessary**

Lock screen password:

"1031"

## 2.Kindle Screen Zones

Kindle screen is divided into these areas:

- Top: Menu
- Left side: Previous page
- Right side: Next page
